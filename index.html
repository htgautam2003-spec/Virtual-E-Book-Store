Journey

Our Story So Far

From a small idea in Patna to India's fastest-growing CS e-book platform.

2022
The Idea is Born
A group of CS students in Patna struggled to afford quality textbooks. They decided to build a platform to change that.
2023
Platform Launched
Virtual E-Book goes live with 200 books across 5 categories. Over 500 students sign up in the first month.
2024
1,000 Books Milestone
The library crosses 1,000 titles with AI, ML, Cybersecurity and Database categories added. 3,000+ students enrolled.
2025
Going National
Expanded to 100+ colleges across 15 Indian states. Launched mobile-friendly downloads and UPI payments.
2026
5K Books & 12K Students
Today we serve 12,000+ students with 5,000+ books, 50+ subjects, and the journey is just getting started.
